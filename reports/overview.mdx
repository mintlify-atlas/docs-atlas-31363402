## Choosing the Right Report

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I want to understand my traffic sources">
    Use the **Attribution Report** to see which referrers, paid ads, and UTM parameters drive traffic to your site. The **UTM Report** provides detailed campaign tracking.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I want to measure conversions">
    The **Funnel Report** helps you track multi-step conversion processes, while the **Goal Report** measures single conversion events. Use **Attribution** to see which sources drive conversions.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I want to understand user behavior">
    The **Journey Report** shows the paths users take through your site. The **Breakdown Report** helps you analyze engagement metrics by different dimensions.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I want to measure user retention">
    Use the **Retention Report** to see cohort-based retention rates and understand how many users return over time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I want to analyze revenue">
    The **Revenue Report** provides comprehensive revenue analytics including trends, geographic distribution, and transaction metrics.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Report Features

All reports in Umami share these common features:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Date Range Selection">
    Choose the time period you want to analyze. Most reports support custom date ranges.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filters">
    Apply filters to focus on specific segments of your traffic (e.g., specific countries, devices, or pages).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Export">
    Download report data for further analysis in CSV or JSON format.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Reports are generated based on privacy-focused analytics data. No personal information is collected or stored.
</Note>
